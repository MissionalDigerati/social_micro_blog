The Path

"Faith is taking the first step even when you don't see the whole staircase."

- Martin Luther King, Jr.

Hi friends,

     You may be thinking, "On the Same Path" has been quiet for awhile! Quiet, but certainly not inactive. Since our last post, God has been very gracious in helping us prepare for our journey into missions. We are grateful to Him for -

  • Friendships: We were able to attend the Mobile Ministry Forum (MMF) early Dec. 2012, a conference focused on using mobile technology in the mission field. What an amazing opportunity to meet a host of like-minded individuals with similar visions. In fact, Johnathan will be joining their steering committee to plan the MMF in 2014.
  • Foundations: We have joined with Commission to Every Nation (CTEN) to assist in the development of our new ministry called Missional Digerati. Through this partnership, we can begin raising financial support and make Missional Digerati a reality.
  • Foresight: God has brought together a few godly men to be on our Accountability Board to give direction to the building of Missional Digerati. They are invaluable in helping define how this ministry can flourish.

     God has been so good in helping us prepare that we are taking the next step of this journey. Johnathan is dedicating his efforts full-time towards this ministry. We hope to share about our journey for Missional Digerati with you in the near future.

     Thanks for listening. Feel free to drop us a line, we like to listen as well! :)

Love,
Johnathan & Candice
Project Director & Ministry Support


Prayer Points

  • Fruitful time at our mission agency's (CTEN) orientation at Kerrville, TX on Mar. 12-14
  • God's direction for MMF Steering Meeting "Huddle" at Chicago, IL on Mar. 25th-27th on how to best serve present network of 50+ Christian ministry and mission agencies
  • We would all be looking forward to when Ps. 117:1-2 is realized, "Praise the Lord, all nations! Extol him, all people. For great is his steadfast love toward us, and the faithfulness of the LORD endures forever. Praise the LORD!"
